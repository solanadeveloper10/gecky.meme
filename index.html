meet Gecky, a laid-back, friendly, big-dreaming gecko from the mind of pepe the frogs creator Matt Furie.

HOW TO BUY

1

Create a wallet

Download the MetaMask extension from the official website and install it in your browser. Fund it by transferring ETH from an exchange or another wallet.

2

Get some Eth

Connect to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap. Open the DEX website and click on "Connect Wallet" and select MetaMask.

3

Go to UniSwap

To swap ETH for GECKY, select ETH in the "from" field and choose GECKY in the "to" field using our contract address. Enter the amount of tokens you want & press swap.

4

Swap for Gecky

Verify your transaction status in MetaMask under the "Activity" tab or on Etherscan using your Ethereum address.
