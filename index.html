meet Gecko, a laid-back, friendly, big-dreaming gecko from the mind of pepe the frogs creator Matt Furie.
Gecko's first appearance was in Matt Furie's book "The Night Riders," published in 2012. Where he was seen hatching from an egg, eager and curious to join hoppy and wat on their nightly adventure. he was then seen again in furie's second book 'Mindviscosity' in 2012.
Whilst attempting to follow Hoppy and Ratty in The night riders, Gecko took a wrong turn and Instead of the familiar, enchanting landscapes of the book, he found himself in the bustling streets of a big city.
